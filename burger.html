Salmon Burgers

Ok. I know this is a sad burger. If I had known I'd be announcing it to the world, I'd have used something other than a potato to take the picture and maybe tried to pretty it up a little. Leave me alone. It tasted yummy.

    Ingredients:

  • Hunk o salmon
  • Bread crumbs
  • Egg
  • White onion
  • Burger buns
  • Swiss cheese
  • Dill pickles
  • Dill
  • Hot sauce
  • Tartar sauce
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Avocado

    What do?

  1. Cut salmon into small cubes
  2. Add 1/4 cup diced white onion, chopped dill, 1/4 cup bread crumbs, egg, salt, and pepper. Massage until mixed.
  3. Form salmon burger patties. If they're too wet, add more bread crumbs and reform.
  4. In a few tbsp oil, cook those baddies. Add cheese toward the end.
  5. Assemble burger with raw onion slices, dill pickles, hot sauce, tartar sauce, tomato, avocado, and lettuce.
  6. Monch.
