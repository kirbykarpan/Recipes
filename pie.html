Venison Pot Pie

I don't claim to be an artist. My Potter (Venison) Pot Pie is... almost art? And it's delicious. It's probably the greatest thing these two hands have ever created.

    Ingredients:

  • 2-3 medium potatoes
  • 3 stalks celery
  • Small can of sweet corn
  • 1 white onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 large carrot
  • Venison roast
  • Vegetable broth
  • Pie crust mix

    What do?

  1. Preheat oven to 375째F
  2. Make the roux! Chop onion, garlic, carrots, and celery. Saute with 1 tbsp oil over medium heat until soft. Set aside
  3. Dice potatoes and toss in 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper and bake for 40 mins at 375째F.
  4. In a few tbsp oil, sear all sides of the venison roast. Bake at 375째F for 10 mins per inch of thickness.
  5. While roast and potatoes finish baking, make pie crusts (top and bottom) and roll out to desired size. Grease pie pan and add bottom crust.
  6. Cut roast into bite-sized pieces. Set aside.
  7. Add potatoes, then roux, then venison to pie. Top with corn from can and mix pie contents carefully and lightly. In the pan that you seared the roast, add 1 cup vegetable broth and 2 tbsp balsamic vinager. Whisk in 2 tbsp flour and cook down to a gravy.
  8. Pour the gravy over the pie, distributing as evenly as possible. Don't overdo it, you don't want a soggy bottom!
  9. Add top pie crust. Pinch edges to seal, and try your damndest to make a design that doesn't look like garbage.
  10. Bake at 375째F for 30-45 minutes. Crust should be golden brown.
  11. Let cool, shove in your pie-hole.
